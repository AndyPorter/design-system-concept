Overview

This manual will contain examples to design, code and write for new casework systems used by staff

The starting point for our casework services and tools is government patterns (from gov.uk, and government departments), components and writing guides, as they are proven patterns, established by thousands to millions of individual users in prototyped and production services.

The context of casework systems is different to professional and public services, so the patterns, components and writing is adapted to utilise those proven patterns in a casework context

You may also use non gov.uk patterns as a starting point to experiment if they would work for caseworkers. Discuss their use with the design community of practice - for help using them, and to help us develop them further. find out how to discuss this with the design community
