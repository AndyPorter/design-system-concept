Page not found concept ideas for a design system

Also knowns as a 404 page, these are pages that the users has navigated to, and do not (have never) existed, for example they have mis-typed in a URL or followed a broken link.

Ensure that the user has a way forward from this page, and a way to report a problem if they followed a legitimate link to the page, from anywhere, for example: one of our services, any public or professional services, an email or text.

404 pages are not for pages that we have moved, permanently or temporarily
